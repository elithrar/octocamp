About

Who am I?

I’m Matt. I work as an ICT Engineer & sometimes-photographer. I’m big on travel, food, books & landscapes, so you’ll see a lot of those things on this blog. I’ve also experience in web design, hosted services and network security (just to round things out).

What do I use?

I started with a Nikon D80, sold my 5D Mark II some time ago, dabbled with a Panasonic GF1 and now shoot exclusively with a Fuji X100. It’s small, it doesn’t need a dedicated bag, has full manual controls and the 35mm effective field-of-view is spot on for how I like to frame shots.
